# Initial Concept
DeepOrbit: Agent-driven Digital Research Assistant for Gemini CLI.

# Product Definition

## Vision
DeepOrbit is a highly customized, agent-driven Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) and Research Assistant system. It supercharges traditional PKM workflows with specialized AI agents that automate deep research, literature translation, content curation, and structural maintenance.

## Core Goal
**Comprehensive Research & PKM Assistant**: A dual-focus system that excels at both high-fidelity academic paper processing (translation, parsing) and automated knowledge base maintenance (summarization, linking).

## Target Audience
- **PKM Enthusiasts**: Individuals building a "second brain" who need automated help organizing and connecting vast amounts of information.
- **Researchers & Curators**: Users who need to ingest, translate, and synthesize academic content or industry news with high signal-to-noise ratios.

## Key Features & Value Proposition
- **Automated Research Pipelines**: Specialized skills like `arxiv-translator` and `marker-pdf` handle the heavy lifting of parsing complex academic content.
- **Smart Knowledge Curation**: Pipelines for AI newsletters, product launches, and research digests ensure users stay updated without manual effort.
- **Structural Integrity**: Features like `ghost-link-fixer` automatically maintain the health of the Obsidian vault by filling gaps in the knowledge graph based on first principles.
- **Hybrid Pipeline**: A seamless bridge between powerful CLI-based automation (Gemini CLI) and the visual/structural benefits of an Obsidian local vault.

## Success Metrics
- **Structural Integrity**: The primary success metric is the automated maintenance of a healthy, interconnected knowledge graph in Obsidian.
- **Automation Efficacy**: The reduction in manual effort for translating papers, summarizing articles, and creating initial project structures.
